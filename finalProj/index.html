about charter#use

❝ Color, you see, was the universal sign of magic. ❞

Getting the right colours for your charts can take a lot of time! This project was created to help :)

Charter#use is a colour generator that focuses on generating qualitative palettes. Generally, this means palettes for data that are made of names rather than numbers. It is best used for chart types that demand use of more than 2 colours to distinguish data. Some examples of such charts include pie charts, doughnut charts and polar area charts!

The algorithm was written to avoid palettes that combined red and green, since that's the most common type of colour-blindness. But of course, it is not the only type of colour-blindness! We highly suggest also running your generated palette through colour-blind tests and tweaking it so that it's as accessible as possible.
